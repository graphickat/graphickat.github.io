Musician Interviews

In January of 2022, I took an immersive podcasting class held at WTJU Charlottesville, taught by station manager Nathan Moore. The course was 8 hours/day day for two weeks. In that time, I received hands-on training for various types of recording equiment & audio editing softwares. For my final project––in a one week time span, I organized, hosted, recorded, & edited a podcast interview with Pavement's drummer, Bob Nastanovich.

Listen to the full episode here


The episode aired on WTJU's online & FM radio stations, inspiring me to continue this project, conducting interviews with other successful music artists. My goal of this project was to demystify the music-making process and encourage my listeners to create art for art's sake.

Interview Excerpt | Shaun & Owain of Panchiko


Each interview was conducted over Zoom. I extracted the audio files from the Zoom recordings, & uploaded them into Adobe Audition. From there, I edited the interviews based on narrative & clarity.
