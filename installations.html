Collection of Portraits | 2022

Representation in creative media validates and empowers underrepresented groups, while dismantling harmful stereotypes and implicit biases.

I created each portrait by hand using the Procreate app on iPad. The full series is displayed in print at the Maxine Platzer Women's Center and featured digitally in Iris Magazine.


Free Flowers | 2021

Free Flowers is an incredibly intricate piece. Drawn by hand using the Procreate app on iPad, I completed this piece over a two-month period using a total of seventy-seven layers.

This piece is on display as a print in Clemons Library at the University of Virginia & featured digitially in Iris Magazine.
